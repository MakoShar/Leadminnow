Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LeadMinnow's lead generation platform.

What exactly does your email marketing service do for interior designers?

We specialize in crafting and sending targeted email campaigns to businesses (like offices, restaurants, hotels, retail spaces, etc.) to generate commercial project leads and job opportunities for interior designers. We aim to connect you with clients who have larger-scale and recurring design needs.

How is this different from just sending out regular emails?

Our approach is strategic and focused. We research and segment potential commercial clients, create compelling content that speaks to their specific needs, and track campaign performance to optimize results. This is not a mass email blast to homeowners.

What kind of commercial clients can I expect to reach?

We target a range of commercial sectors based on your expertise and preferences, such as offices, retail spaces, hospitality (hotels, restaurants), healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and more. We'll work with you to define your ideal commercial client profile.

What kind of results can I expect?

While results can vary, our goal is to generate qualified leads and ultimately secure commercial design projects for you. We focus on quality over quantity, aiming for genuine business opportunities. Leadminnow also offers website building services to ensure you have a professional platform to convert these leads.

What is your guarantee?

We guarantee that you will secure at least 5 new commercial clients/jobs within the first 3 months of using our email marketing service. If we don't meet this target, you pay us nothing.

Do I need to provide email lists or do the emailing myself?

No, you don't need to do any of that! Leadminnow handles the entire process. We utilize our own strategies to build targeted lists of commercial contacts and manage all aspects of the email campaigns, from design and sending to tracking and optimization.

Besides email marketing, what other services does Leadminnow offer that could help me get more commercial clients?

Leadminnow offers a range of services designed to boost your commercial client acquisition. This includes:
* Meta Ads: Targeted advertising on platforms like Facebook and Instagram to reach commercial businesses.
* Website Building: Creating a professional online presence to showcase your portfolio and services to potential commercial clients.
* 24/7 Support AI Chatbot: Engaging visitors on your website and capturing leads from interested commercial businesses.
* Animated Ads & Animated Interior Design: Creating visually compelling content to showcase your design capabilities and attract attention from commercial clients online and in email campaigns
