Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Meta advertising services.

Meta advertising refers to paid promotional campaigns run across Meta's family of apps, including Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and the Audience Network. These platforms offer sophisticated targeting options based on demographics, interests, behaviors, and more, allowing businesses to reach specific audiences with tailored messaging.

Initial results can typically be seen within the first 2-4 weeks as the campaigns gather data and our team makes optimizations. However, the most significant improvements usually occur after 1-3 months as we refine targeting, creative elements, and bidding strategies based on performance data. Long-term campaigns tend to perform better as they benefit from accumulated learnings and optimizations.

Businesses across virtually all industries can benefit from Meta advertising, from e-commerce and retail to professional services, B2B companies, local businesses, and more. The key is developing a strategy aligned with your specific business goals and target audience. Meta's extensive reach and targeting capabilities make it effective for both B2C and B2B marketing objectives, including brand awareness, lead generation, website traffic, and sales.

We create and manage all Meta ad formats, including image ads, video ads, carousel ads, collection ads, instant experience ads, stories ads, and more. Our team recommends the most effective formats based on your specific business goals, target audience, and creative assets. We can also develop custom creative assets tailored to each format to maximize engagement and conversions.

Our management fee covers strategy development, campaign setup, audience targeting, ad creation, ongoing optimization, performance monitoring, A/B testing, and regular reporting. It also includes account management, strategic guidance, and technical support. The ad spend (the money that goes directly to Meta for displaying your ads) is separate from our management fee and is paid directly to Meta through your advertising account.

Yes, we recommend a minimum monthly ad spend of $1,500 to generate meaningful results. This allows for sufficient testing and optimization across different audiences and ad creatives. However, we can work with businesses at various stages and budgets, and we're happy to discuss your specific situation to find the most effective approach for your goals and resources.

We typically recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to allow sufficient time for campaign optimization and to see meaningful results. However, we offer flexible terms and month-to-month options after the initial period. We're confident in the value we provide and don't believe in locking clients into lengthy contracts. Our average client relationship lasts over 18 months because of the results we deliver.

We implement comprehensive tracking using the Meta Pixel, Conversions API, and UTM parameters to accurately measure campaign performance. Our reporting includes key metrics such as impressions, reach, engagement, click-through rates, conversion rates, cost per acquisition, and return on ad spend. We also integrate with your analytics platforms (like Google Analytics) and CRM systems to track the full customer journey and attribute results accurately.

We utilize Meta's full range of targeting options, including demographic targeting (age, gender, location, language), interest-based targeting, behavioral targeting, custom audiences (website visitors, customer lists, app users), lookalike audiences, and retargeting. Our approach combines these targeting methods strategically to reach the most relevant audiences at each stage of the customer journey, from awareness to consideration to conversion.

We stay at the forefront of privacy changes and platform updates, implementing best practices to maintain campaign performance. This includes using Meta's Conversions API alongside the Meta Pixel for more reliable tracking, leveraging server-side tracking where appropriate, focusing on aggregated event measurement, and developing strategies that are less dependent on individual user tracking. We also emphasize first-party data collection and utilize Meta's AI-powered targeting capabilities to reach relevant audiences while respecting privacy preferences.
